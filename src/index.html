Exii Research Group

We're a group of human-computer interaction researchers investigating fundamental characteristics of human input and novel forms of interaction for current and future computing form factors. Our work is diverse, covering input methods like pen, touch, mouse, tangibles, mid-air gestures, and whole-body input, for everything from desktops, mobile phones, tablets, and wearable devices, to large displays, virtual reality, augmented reality, and fabrication. Find out who we are and what we do.
